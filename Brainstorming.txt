First model: (reserve problem) maximize energy traded in the wholesale market while minimizing the energy traded in spot market.
Assumption: whole sale price < spot price (most of the time)

Second model: 
- in case of over-capacity, predict the upper interval limit of expected selling price in the sopt market.
- in case of under-capacity, predict the lower interval limit of expected selling price in the spot market
